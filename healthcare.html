Computer-Enhanced Prosthetic Limbs

Computing has revolutionized the field of prosthetics by enabling the design and manufacturing of more functional, customized, and intuitive limbs. Computer-aided technologies improve every stage of development, from better design and fit to more natural control and sensory feedback.

Disease Identification and Tracking

Recent improvements in computing have allowed the diagnosis side of healthcare to rapidly improve, increasing the overall accuracy of healthcare, and thus improving the likelihood of a successful treatment plan. It has also allowed us to track diseases more effectively, as seen with the COVID-19 pandemic, where Canadian company BlueDot managed to correctly predict 'hotspots' of the virus, using an AI-led approach, analysing frequent travel routes and scouting internet chatrooms for individuals discussing symptoms consistent with the COVID-19 virus.
